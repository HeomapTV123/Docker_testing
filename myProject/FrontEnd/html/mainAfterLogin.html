Chefs

Gordon Ramsay

Gordon Ramsay is a world-famous Scottish chef, restaurateur, TV personality, and writer known for his fiery temper, culinary excellence (holding multiple Michelin stars), and hit TV shows like Hell's Kitchen, Kitchen Nightmares, and MasterChef

Julia Child

Julia Child (1912-2004) was a groundbreaking American chef, author, and TV personality famous for popularizing French cuisine in the U.S. through her book Mastering the Art of French Cooking (1961) and her TV show The French Chef (1963).

Guy Fieri

Guy Fieri is a famous American chef, restaurateur, author, and Food Network star known for his spiky blonde hair and high-energy personality, famous for shows like Diners, Drive-Ins, and Dives and Guy's Grocery Games

David Chang

David Chang is a renowned Korean-American chef, restaurateur, media personality, and founder of the influential Momofuku restaurant group, known for his innovative, Asian-inspired cuisine, and James Beard awards. He's a Time 100 honoree, author, podcast host (The Dave Chang Show), and creator of Netflix's Ugly Delicious
